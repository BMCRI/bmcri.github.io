Department of Ophthalmology

Committed to providing the best services in the treatment of eye diseases. Offering a complete range of examination, treatment, and surgical options.

Vision

To lead innovation in patient care, developing Retinal, Glaucoma, and Cornea units, along with a functional eye bank.

Mission

To provide a complete range of surgical options using the latest technologies, ensuring high-quality service for each patient.

Clinical Services

  • General Ophthalmology & Vision Tests.
  • Glaucoma Treatment & School Health Check-ups.
  • SICS and Phacoemulsification for cataract surgery.

Equipment

  • Auto Refractometer & Non-contact Tonometer.
  • A-scan Ultrasonography & Nd-Yag Laser.
  • Surgical Microscopes.

Outreach

  • 24/7 Emergency care for trauma.
  • Functional eye collection center.
  • Regular eye camps in nearby villages.
