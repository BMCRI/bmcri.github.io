Department of General Medicine

The backbone of the hospital, providing comprehensive care for a wide range of adult diseases. Dedicated to excellence in patient care, medical education, and clinical research.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Internal Medicine, providing holistic care and fostering continuous learning and research.

Mission

To provide high-quality services and train students in the art and science of medicine with empathy.

Infrastructure

  • OPD: Daily services by experienced physicians.
  • Wards: Male & Female wards with 24/7 monitoring.
  • ICCU: State-of-the-art Critical Care units.

Clinical Focus

  • Infectious and Non-communicable diseases.
  • Diabetes, Hypertension & Geriatric care.
  • ECG, 2D-Echo & Pulmonary Function Test (PFT).

Academics

Emphasis on bedside clinics and skill development.

  • Regular clinical case discussions & seminars.
  • Departmental Library.
