Respiratory Medicine

Dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of respiratory system diseases. We play a pivotal role in national health programs and medical education.

Vision

To be a premier center for respiratory care, providing advanced clinical services and fostering a strong academic environment.

Mission

To provide comprehensive teaching and coordinate the National Tuberculosis Elimination Programme (NTEP).

Clinical Services

  • Care: Respiratory ICU & Indoor Wards.
  • Diagnostic Pleural Procedures.
  • Pulmonary Function Testing.

NTEP

  • National Tuberculosis Elimination Programme.
  • Nodal Center for NTEP coordination.
  • Specialized Tuberculosis Clinic.
  • Managing TB cases and activities.

Academics

  • Lectures & clinical teaching for MBBS/Interns.
  • Bedside clinical teaching & OPD training.
  • Case discussions and seminars.
