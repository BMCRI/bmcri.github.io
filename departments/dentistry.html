Department of Dentistry

Concerns the prevention and treatment of oral diseases, including teeth, supporting structures, and soft tissues. We provide patient care to the underprivileged using state-of-the-art technology.

Vision

To empower students regarding Dental sciences, their systemic interconnectedness, and various treatment modalities.

Mission

To provide comprehensive care for oral diseases and malformations, focusing on early cancer detection and community service.

Specialties

  • Orthodontics, Endodontics, & Prosthodontics.
  • Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery.
  • Focus: Early detection of Oral Cancer.

Patient Care

  • Treatment of oral diseases & tooth misalignment.
  • Correction of jaw malformations & cleft palate.
  • Understanding systemic manifestations of oral issues.

Infra & Learning

  • State-of-the-art Dental Chairs & X-ray Radiology.
  • Dedicated Teaching Areas & Small group sessions.
  • 15-day clinical posting for students.
