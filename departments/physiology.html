Department of Physiology

The study of functions of the human body and regulation of systems. Providing comprehensive knowledge of integrated organ functions to understand health and disease.

Vision

To provide best-in-class training by adopting relevant methods and contributing new knowledge through need-based research.

Mission

To equip students with skills to interpret experimental data, distinguishing between normal and abnormal to treat diseases.

Labs & Research

  • Dedicated Research Laboratory for physiological studies.
  • Hematology and Clinical Physiology Labs.
  • Conducting experiments & data interpretation.

Teaching

  • Integrated teaching of organ systems.
  • Computer Assisted Learning (CAL) Facility.
  • Demonstration rooms with AV aids.
  • Understanding adaptations to environment.

Resources

  • Departmental Library with updated books.
  • Facilities for faculty and student research.
  • Focus on identifying normal vs abnormal.
