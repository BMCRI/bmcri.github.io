Department of Anaesthesiology

The lifeline of the surgical wing, dedicated to ensuring patient safety and comfort. We provide comprehensive perioperative care, manage the Surgical Intensive Care Unit (SICU), and offer compassionate pain management services using the latest advancements in anesthesia safety.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in perioperative medicine and critical care, ensuring the highest standards of patient safety and monitoring.

Mission

To provide safe anesthesia for all specialties, train competent professionals in emergency management, and deliver compassionate pain relief.

Clinical Services

  • General, Regional, Paediatric & Geriatric Anaesthesia.
  • Labour Analgesia & Obstetric Anaesthesia.
  • PAC Clinic: Pre-operative optimization.
  • Pain Clinic: Chronic pain management.

Infrastructure & ICU

  • Modular OTs with advanced workstations.
  • SICU: 24/7 Critical Care support.
  • Video Laryngoscopes & Fibreoptic Bronchoscopes.
  • High-end Ventilators & Infusion Pumps.

Academics

Focusing on skill acquisition and emergency management.

  • BLS & ACLS Training Workshops.
  • Simulation-based learning.
  • Departmental Library & Case discussions.
