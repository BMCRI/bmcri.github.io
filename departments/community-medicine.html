Community Medicine

Preparing graduates to become effective Community Physicians by percolating the importance of "Good Health" at the grass-root level and promoting higher education standards.

Vision

To provide comprehensive community health services, promote research, and foster life-long learning and leadership skills.

Mission

Utilize innovative methods for student-centered learning and liaison with institutions to exchange knowledge.

Infrastructure

  • Centers: RHTC Chitrasani, UHTC Rabari Vas.
  • Field Stations: For extensive fieldwork.
  • Research Labs: Epidemiology & Biostatistics.

Outreach

  • Family Adoption: 1000+ families covered.
  • Camps: 450+ screening camps organized.
  • Villages: Serving Kushkal, Moriya, Malana, etc.

Academics

  • Small Group Discussions & Self-Directed Learning.
  • Field Visits & Industrial Visits.
  • Department Museum on public health history.
