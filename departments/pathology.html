Department of Pathology

The bridge between clinical medicine and basic science. Dedicated to linking etiology and pathogenesis to provide optimal patient care while excelling in education and diagnostics.

Vision

To provide high-quality education and diagnostic services, promoting research in pathology.

Mission

To produce doctors able to link pathogenesis with care, excelling through recent advances and self-improvement.

Diagnostics

  • 24/7 Services: Blood Bank & Central Lab.
  • Histopathology & Cytopathology labs.
  • Fully automated machines & staining units.

Equipment

  • Decahead, Pentahead & Trinocular microscopes.
  • Microscopes interfaced with TV screens.
  • Grossing station with recording facility.

Academics

  • Linking etiology to patient care.
  • Museum with 309 mounted specimens.
  • 2 Demonstration rooms with AV aids.
  • Air-conditioned library & Research lab.
