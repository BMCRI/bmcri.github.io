Forensic Medicine & Toxicology

Providing comprehensive medicolegal education through integrated group dynamics. We maintain state-of-the-art facilities for legal medicine education and expert opinion.

Vision

To facilitate group dynamics, integrate with other departments, and provide top-tier medicolegal education.

Mission

To deliver conceptual knowledge through interactive lectures, emphasizing Early Clinical Exposure (ECE) and Self-Directed Learning.

Infrastructure

  • Mortuary: 4-body cold storage & Postmortem facility.
  • Museum: Wet specimens, ballistics & anthropology.
  • Demonstration halls with LCD projectors.

Medicolegal

  • Autopsy techniques & Drunkenness examination.
  • Injury Certificates, MCCD, & Rape Victim examination.
  • Age Estimation & Medical Law procedures.

Academics

  • Facilities to teach 200 students per batch.
  • on ECE, SDL, and Role Play.
  • Library (>85 textbooks) & reading room.
  • Anthropology instruments for stature studies.
