Department of General Surgery

Committed to providing affordable, latest surgical treatments with a focus on minimally invasive options to ensure early recovery and less pain for our patients.

Vision

To promote laparoscopic surgery to advanced levels, establish a state-of-the-art endoscopy unit, and start Post Graduate programs.

Teaching

To provide integrated knowledge and surgical skills, emphasizing self-learning, ethics, and practical application.

Infrastructure

  • OPD: Ground Floor 'B' Block with teaching rooms.
  • OTs: Modular OT in 'D' block + Minor OTs.
  • SICU: Dedicated Surgical ICU near Emergency.

Services & Future

  • Minimally invasive & Advanced Laparoscopic surgeries.
  • Endoscopic diagnostic & therapeutic procedures.
  • Planned: 3 new Modular OTs & Endoscopy room.

Academics

Integrated teaching based on NMC curriculum.

  • Central Library (42 book types, 10 journals).
  • Dedicated teaching rooms for clinical exposure.
  • Focus on ethical and moral standards.
