Department of Dermatology

Aspiring to be a center of excellence, delivering high-quality education, research, and compassionate patient care. Committed to extending patient-centered care and ensuring dermatological health.

Vision

To develop advanced infrastructure including lasers, phototherapy, and specialty clinics for leprosy, vitiligo, and psoriasis.

Mission

To provide comprehensive education and holistic, evidence-based treatment while promoting skin health awareness.

Services

  • OPD: D Block (Mon-Sat).
  • Inpatient: 10 beds (5 Male + 5 Female).
  • Holistic, patient-centered care.

Advanced Care

  • Specialty clinics: Leprosy, Vitiligo, Psoriasis.
  • Phototherapy & Radiofrequency treatments.
  • Future: Lasers for scar/hair/tattoo removal.

Academics

  • Comprehensive education for students & residents.
  • Equipping students with skills to excel.
  • Promoting community skin health awareness.
