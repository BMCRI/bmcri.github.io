Department of Pharmacology

Imparting knowledge on the therapeutic basis of essential drugs. We strive to understand disease mechanisms and drug action for better patient care.

Vision

To be a Department of Excellence focusing on the rational, safe use of life-saving drugs and Pharmacovigilance.

Mission

To deliver education via innovative methods, treat ADRs through PvPI, and educate the community on safe medication.

Key Initiatives

  • PvPI: Monitoring Adverse Drug Reactions (ADR).
  • Outreach: Educating public on safe medicine use.
  • Promoting rational use of antibiotics.

Academics

  • Emphasizing critical thinking & self-reliance.
  • Virtual Lab Training & CAL.
  • Analytical skill development.
  • Collaborative and translational research.

Infrastructure

  • Experimental Pharmacology Labs.
  • Computer Aided Learning (CAL) Lab.
  • Departmental Library & PvPI Centre.
