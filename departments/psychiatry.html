Department of Psychiatry

Leading efforts in understanding, diagnosing, and treating psychiatric disorders. We provide state-of-the-art care to improve community behavioral health.

Vision

To prepare clinicians and lead efforts to prevent disorders while guiding community psychological health.

Mission

To provide distinctive, quality mental healthcare, especially to the underprivileged, adhering to compliances.
