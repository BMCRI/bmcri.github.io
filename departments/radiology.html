Department of Radio-Diagnosis

The diagnostic hub of the institute, equipped with state-of-the-art imaging technologies. Providing 24/7 diagnostic and interventional services for accurate treatment.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in diagnostic imaging and intervention, integrating technology with clinical expertise.

Mission

To provide high-quality imaging services and train future radiologists in modern modalities and safety protocols.

Modalities

  • Advanced: Multi-slice CT & High-field MRI.
  • Digital Radiography (DR) & Fluoroscopy.
  • Ultrasound & Color Doppler.

Clinical Services

  • 24/7 Emergency Imaging Services.
  • Interventional Radiology (Biopsies, Drainages).
  • Mammography for Cancer Screening.

Academics

  • PACS & High-Res Reporting Monitors.
  • Daily Film Reading & Radiation Safety Training.
  • Library & E-Learning resources.
