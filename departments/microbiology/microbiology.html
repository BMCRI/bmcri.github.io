Department of Microbiology

The Microbiology department is managed by qualified faculty and technicians, committed to high-end teaching, training, and diagnostic services. We create a conducive environment for research and paramedical training.

Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence for Microbiology Practice, Education, Research & Clinical Service.

Mission

To provide high-end diagnostic services and create a conducive environment for research and training.

Diagnostic Services

  • Central Lab: Bacteriology, Serology, Virology, Parasitology, Mycology.
  • Molecular Lab: RT-PCR for COVID-19, Hepatitis B & C Viral Load.
  • Dedicated Research Laboratory.

Programs & Safety

  • National Programs: NVHCP, ICTC (HIV), RNTCP (TB), IDSP.
  • Hospital Infection Prevention & Control program.
  • Antibiotic Stewardship Program.

Academics

  • NMC curriculum focused on infectious disease history.
  • Interactive lectures, tutorials & Self-directed learning.
  • Museum with mounted specimens.
  • Departmental library (>100 books).
