Department of Biochemistry

Bridging the gap between fundamental science and clinical medicine. We equip students to diagnose diseases through analysis and interpretation of clinical investigations.

Vision

To help students correlate clinical biochemistry with modern medicine, enabling accurate disease diagnosis through lab analysis.

Mission

To provide diagnostic services at the Central Lab and enhance student learning through clinical postings and internship experience.

Clinical Services

  • Central Lab: Diagnostic services for General Hospital.
  • Diagnosis of diseases based on lab investigation.
  • Application of laboratory medicine in practice.

Academic Training

Understanding the chemical basis of life and disease.

  • Human Biochemistry for 1st Year MBBS.
  • Elective clinical postings & Internship training.
  • Hands-on experience in clinical biochemistry.

Facilities

  • Student Practical Laboratories.
  • Demonstration Rooms.
  • Departmental Library.
