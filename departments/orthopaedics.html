Department of Orthopaedics

Dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of musculoskeletal injuries and diseases. We provide world-class care for trauma, joint disorders, and spinal conditions.

Vision

To be a center of excellence providing advanced interventions like arthroplasty and arthroscopy, training surgeons with high ethical standards.

Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality trauma care, integrate modern technology, and promote research in orthopaedic surgery.

Clinical Services

  • Trauma: 24/7 care for complex fractures & poly-trauma.
  • Joints: Hip & Knee Replacement Surgery.
  • Spine Surgery & Paediatric Orthopaedics.

Infrastructure

  • Modular OTs with C-Arm & Arthroscopy units.
  • Dedicated Wards, Plaster Room & Dressing Room.
  • Integrated Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation unit.

Academics

  • Focus on hands-on skill acquisition.
  • Surgical Skills Workshops (Fixation, Suturing).
  • Daily Trauma Meetings & Case Discussions.
  • Departmental Library & Research.
