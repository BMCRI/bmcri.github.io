Department of ENT

Providing comprehensive care for diseases related to the Ear, Nose, Throat, and Head & Neck surgery. Committed to excellence in patient care, audiological services, and medical education.

Vision

To be a center of excellence providing microsurgery and endoscopic solutions while training future specialists.

Mission

To provide high-quality diagnostic services, conduct hearing research, and offer rehabilitation for speech disorders.

Infrastructure

  • Facilities: Daily OPD & Inpatient Wards.
  • OT: Microscopes & High-definition Endoscopes.
  • Audiology: Sound-treated rooms & Speech therapy.

Clinical Care

  • Tonsillectomy, Adenoidectomy, Tympanoplasty.
  • FESS (Endoscopic Sinus Surgery) & Mastoidectomy.
  • Clinics for Vertigo, Cancer (Head/Neck) & Voice.

Academics

  • Training in diagnostic and surgical skills.
  • Temporal Bone Dissection Workshops.
  • Departmental Library & Museum.
  • Clinical case presentations & Journal Clubs.
