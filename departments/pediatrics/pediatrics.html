Department of Pediatrics

Providing comprehensive treatment to all pediatric patients from birth to 18 years of age. We work integrated with different departments to provide holistic care.

Vision

To teach undergraduates the basics of pediatric conditions, prevention, and treatment.

Facilities

Operating four units with specialized clinics, critical care units, and wards in "D" block.

Clinical Services

  • Comprehensive care (Birth to 18 years).
  • Immunization services.
  • OPD: Two general rooms & Special weekday clinics.

Infrastructure

  • Critical Care: PICU & SNCU.
  • Wards: 3 wards on 3rd floor & Isolation room.
  • Organized into Four Units.

Academics

  • Focus on prevention and treatment education.
  • Understanding basics of medical conditions.
  • Prevention strategies.
  • Departmental Library.
